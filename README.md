## Data Transformation

Data cleaned and transformed using **Power Query** and Excel formulas:

- Removed missing or inconsistent entries
- Extracted **Order Year** from dates
- Formatted date and currency columns
- Grouped and summarized by Region, Category, and Year
- Calculated total sales, product count, and average sales per year

## Conclusion and Recommendation

### ðŸŽ¯ **Conclusion**

- The Superstore earned **$2.26M** in total revenue from 2015 to 2018.
- **Office Supplies** sold the most units, but **Technology** brought in the most revenue.
- The **West** region led in sales, with consistent growth in Technology.
- **2017** recorded the highest average sales, marking it as a strong year.

---

### ðŸ’¡ **Recommendations**

- **Focus** more promotional/inventory efforts on the **Technology** category.
- **Explore** why Office Supplies sell more units but generate less revenue.
- **Investigate** the dip in average sales after **2017**.
- **Expand** successful strategies from the **West** region to **South** and **Central**.
- **Maintain** data-driven decision-making by refreshing this dashboard regularly.
